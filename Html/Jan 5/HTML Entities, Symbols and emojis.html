These are some of the Entities

Some characters are reserved in HTML.
They are coded as &entity_name or number.

  • < > < >
  • &
  • "
  • '
  • ¢
  • £
  • ¥
  • €
  • ©
  • ®

These are some of the symbols

Symbols that are not present on your keyboard can also be added by using entities.
Some other symbols are

  • euro - €
  • forall- ∀
  • summation - ∑
  • epsilon - Ε
  • delta - δ
  • Copyright - ©
  • Registered - ®
  • Arrows - ↑ ↓ ← →
  • Cards - ♠ ♣ ⋄ ♥
  • rupee - ₨
